Dastan Satpayev

12 august 2008, Football player

  • Born in Almaty, Dastan Satpayev joined the Kairat academy at 8 years old.
  • Professional footballer who plays as a forward for Kazakhstan Premier League club Kairat
  • Satpayev is a youth international for Kazakhstan, first playing with the under-17 since October 2023,[15] scoring 4 goals in 10 games and eventually captaining the side.
  • In February 2025, Kairat announced that he agreed a transfer to English Premier League club Chelsea.
  • He will join Premier League club Chelsea in Summer 2026.
